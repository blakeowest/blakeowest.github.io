About Me

In the beginning, I wanted to learn to code because of an app idea. Now I'm learning because of a passion. I've always had a knack for computers and technology, and was always able to learn new things quickly. But it wasn't until I was in college when I started thinking of building a career in the tech field. I graduated with my Bachelors in Hospitality Management from Florida State University in May of 2016. I have always been in the customer service industry so I saw it fitting to continue that path. But soon after graduating, I wanted to try my hand at coding. So in early 2017, I began my coding adventure and haven't looked back. I continue to learn new languages and will always keep learning, as I think this is a never ending technology (which is awesome). I love the gratification of putting all the pieces together to build a wesbite or an application and of course, the problem solving that comes with it.
